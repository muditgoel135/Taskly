# Taskly

**Taskly** is a modern AI-powered task planner designed to help students stay focused, organised, and productive. Built with a clean, distraction-free dark theme, Taskly combines **AI-assisted scheduling**, **smart reminders**, and **automatic distraction blocking** to make studying more efficient.

Key Features:

* **AI-Assisted Planning** – turn tasks and deadlines into a realistic schedule.
* **Smart Reminders** – notifications on phone and laptop before deadlines.
* **Distraction Blocking** – automatically blocks games, social media, and other distractions during focus sessions.
* **Habit & Goal Tracking** – track your progress and build positive study routines.
* **Offline Mode** – reliable planning even without internet.
* **Integrations** – sync with Google Calendar, school portals, and other apps.
* **Privacy-First** – minimal data collection with secure encryption.

Taskly helps students cut through distractions, plan smarter, and achieve more — turning study time into future success.
